Whats Oppo Stock ROM and its Advantage

The Stock ROM (Mobile OS) is the Most Stable ROM released by the device manufacturers depending on the device model and Android version. However, the Stock ROM donâ€™t offer many customizations or features for the device.

Advantages of Oppo Stock ROM
